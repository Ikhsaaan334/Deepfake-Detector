About Us

Our Mission

Our project is dedicated to developing a robust and comprehensive digital forensic system. As manipulation techniques evolve beyond facial swaps to include body movements and environmental alterations, we aim to implement a sophisticated detection pipeline that analyzes every element within a media frame.

By conducting a rigorous comparative analysis of five industry-leading Object Detection algorithms—YOLOv8, SSD, Faster R-CNN, RetinaNet, and EfficientDet—we are identifying the optimal engine for precise, full-frame object localization. Our mission culminates in the integration of this superior model into a streamlined application, empowering users to upload media for automated scanning and receive definitive probability assessments regarding the authenticity of their images and videos.

The Technology

The core of our system relies on advanced Object Detection Algorithms, which serve as the essential first phase for mapping and localizing elements within a digital file. To ensure the highest standards of accuracy and speed, we conducted an exhaustive comparative analysis of five leading architectures: YOLOv8, SSD, Faster R-CNN, RetinaNet, and EfficientDet.

These models allow the system to scan entire frames efficiently, creating a precise map of objects that the subsequent classification model uses to detect anomalies. The highest-performing algorithm is then integrated into our software, enabling a seamless workflow where the application automatically processes user uploads, localizes all visual components, and generates a precise probability score to indicate the likelihood of manipulation.
